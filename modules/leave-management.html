Module 07

Leave management software with policy control and smoother approvals

FuzionHR Leave Management helps businesses apply leave rules consistently while giving employees and managers clear visibility into balances and approvals.

Problems the module solves

  • Manual leave tracking creates balance errors and approval delays.
  • Employees lack visibility into remaining leave and policy rules.
  • Managers approve without a full view of team availability.
  • Payroll and attendance calculations become difficult to reconcile.

Core capabilities

  • Policy-based leave setup
  • Accruals, balances and request workflows
  • Manager approvals and escalations
  • Employee self-service visibility
  • Integration with attendance and payroll workflows

Stronger transparency
