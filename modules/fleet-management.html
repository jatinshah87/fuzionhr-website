Why fleet processes become messy

  • Vehicle assignment records are hard to trace consistently.
  • Maintenance schedules and supporting documents live in separate files.
  • Operational ownership is unclear across teams.
  • Transport-related expenses are difficult to reconcile.

What the module manages

  • Vehicle and assignment records
  • Usage and operational ownership visibility
  • Maintenance reminders and document tracking
  • Support for related reimbursement workflows
  • Greater discipline across movement-related operations
