Module 05

Probation evaluation software for timely reviews and cleaner confirmation decisions

FuzionHR helps teams run structured probation checkpoints so employee confirmations are based on visible feedback, not delayed memory.

Why businesses need this module

  • Probation reviews are often missed or delayed.
  • Manager feedback stays informal and undocumented.
  • HR lacks visibility into readiness and role fit.
  • Confirmation decisions become inconsistent across teams.

Key module outcomes

  • Scheduled review checkpoints
  • Manager feedback collection
  • Role fit and readiness tracking
  • Confirmation, extension or action workflows
  • Audit trail for probation decisions

Better accountability

Managers review employees on time with clear prompts and structure.

Fairer decisions

HR can support confirmation calls with recorded evidence and context.

Stronger employee development

Early feedback becomes a growth tool rather than a late-stage surprise.

Induction

Carry role expectations from day-one orientation into review milestones.

Performance Management

Move confirmed employees into longer-cycle review frameworks.
