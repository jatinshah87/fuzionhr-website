What goes wrong without a performance system

  • Feedback gets delayed until the end of the cycle.
  • Development discussions lack visibility and follow-through.

Performance features that matter

  • Appraisal-cycle setup and review scheduling
  • Goal and expectation tracking
  • Manager feedback and review documentation
  • Employee self-input where relevant
  • Development-focused review continuity
