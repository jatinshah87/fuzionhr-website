Module 09

Visitor management software for safer, smoother workplace access

FuzionHR Visitor Management helps organizations register guests, route approvals and improve entry visibility without manual front-desk complexity.

Key visitor-management pain points

  • Guest logs are still paper-based or difficult to search later.
  • Approvals are informal and security teams lack context.
  • Reception experiences feel unstructured and slow.
  • There is limited visibility into visitor history and movement.

Capabilities for workplace control

  • Visitor pre-registration and check-in
  • Approval routing to hosts or departments
  • Entry tracking and historical visibility
  • Reception workflow standardization
  • Stronger audit trail for guest movement
