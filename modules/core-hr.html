Core HR software that creates a reliable people-data foundation

FuzionHR Core HR gives businesses one source of truth for employee records, organizational structure, documents and essential HR workflows.

What Core HR helps you solve

  • Scattered employee information across spreadsheets and folders.
  • Weak visibility into reporting structure, departments and locations.
  • Document chaos during audits, transfers and policy checks.
  • Inconsistent HR data feeding payroll and downstream processes.

Key capabilities

  • Employee master and profile records
  • Department, branch and reporting hierarchy setup
  • Document storage and HR record governance
  • Role-based access and data control
  • Custom fields for business-specific workflows

Operational clarity

Keep employee information current and accessible to the right people.

Payroll readiness

Scalable administration

Standardize people records before the business grows more complex.
