What induction should accomplish

  • Introduce culture, policies and workplace expectations clearly.
  • Clarify role outcomes, team responsibilities and reporting lines.
  • Cover compliance, safety or business-specific protocols.
  • Help managers and HR measure readiness early.

Module capabilities

  • Induction schedules and session planning
  • Checklist-based orientation tracking
  • Role and team introduction workflows
  • Policy and compliance acknowledgement
  • Readiness confirmation and follow-through
